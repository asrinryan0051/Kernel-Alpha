About Kernel Alpha

Kernel Alpha is a web application designed to analyse & predict important questions based on the syllabus & PYO's of the CSE department of Anna University under Regulation 2021.

Our app utilizes the specialized CORe Algorithm (CONCEPT ORIENTED RETRIEVAL ENGINE) designed by Asrin Ryan C to predict examination patterns.
